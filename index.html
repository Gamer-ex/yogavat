About Us

Yogavat Foundation is a Not-for-Profit organization. Registered as Section 8 Company. Main objectives are to promote National integration, awareness of cultural, spiritual, and traditional knowledge of our nation and to provide socio-economic empowerment to the weaker sections of the society and to spread awareness about the glorious knowledge tradition of our nation, its history, philosophy, and religion, thereby to promote patriotism through affiliated organizations and associations organised through community participation.
